How to Make Fluffy Pancakes

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • vanilla to taste
  • pinch of salt
  1. Mix dry ingredients in large bowl.
  2. In a seperate bowl. Measure out milk and add egg and vanilla. Mix together.
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry bowl with dry ingredients. Mix until all lumps are gone.
  4. Cover and let set for at least one hour.
  5. Cook and enjoy!
