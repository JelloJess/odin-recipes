Chocolate Cake

How to Make the Best Chocolate Cake

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup soured milk (add 1 tsp of vinegar)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup strong black coffee
  • vanilla
  1. Mix dry ingredients together in large bowl.
  2. Mix wet ingredients together in medium bowl.
  3. Mix wet and dry ingredients together.
  4. Put in parchement lined cake pan>
  5. Bake at 350 for 50 minutes.
